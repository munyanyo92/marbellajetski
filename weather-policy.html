Weather & Cancellation Policy

Your safety comes first. Here's how we handle weather-related changes to your booking.

Safety-First Approach

At Marbella JetSki, every decision starts with safety. We monitor weather conditions continuously using official maritime sources: AEMET (Spanish Meteorological Agency) and Puertos del Estado (State Ports sea-state data).

Who decides? — The final call on whether conditions are safe is made by our base skipper (the on-duty maritime officer). This decision is non-negotiable and always taken in the interest of participant safety.

Cancellation Criteria

Activities may be cancelled or postponed when any of the following thresholds are met:

Wind Speed
> 20 kn (Force 5+)
Sustained or gusting
Sea State
> 1.0 m wave height
Significant wave height (Hs)
Thunderstorm
Any lightning risk
Within 10 NM radius
Visibility
< 1 km
Fog, heavy rain, or haze

Even if conditions appear calm on shore, sea-state forecasts or rapid weather changes may make it unsafe to operate. The skipper's decision is final.

How We Notify You

Evening before (by 21:00)

If the forecast is clearly adverse, we contact you via WhatsApp or phone to discuss rescheduling.

Morning of activity (by 09:00)

Final check of real-time conditions. If borderline, we inform you and may offer a delayed start.

On-site assessment

If you're already at the beach and conditions change, the skipper may pause or cancel mid-session. Unused time is refunded or credited.

Your Options If We Cancel

When we cancel due to weather, you always have a choice:

Reschedule

Move your booking to another date during your stay — subject to availability, no extra charge.

Open Voucher

Receive a voucher valid for 12 months. Use it yourself or give it to someone. Transferable.

Full Refund

100% refund to your original payment method, processed within 5–7 business days.

No-questions-asked guarantee: If we cancel for weather, you will never lose your money. Full refund, voucher, or free reschedule — your choice.

Cancellations by You

If you choose to cancel and the weather is within safe limits, our standard cancellation policy applies:

  • > 48 hours before: full refund
  • 24–48 hours before: 50% refund or free reschedule
  • < 24 hours / no-show: no refund
