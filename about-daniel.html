Who We Are

Marbella JetSki — Watersports Since 1998

Marbella JetSki is a family-owned watersports company operated by STIERS E HIJOS S.L., based on the Costa del Sol. We have been offering premium water activities in Marbella for over 25 years.

From jet ski excursions and boat trips to paddleboarding, kayaking, and private racing lessons, our goal is to deliver safe, exciting, and memorable experiences on the Mediterranean Sea.

We operate with full licences and insurance, hold ISO 9001 (Quality) and ISO 14001 (Environmental) certifications, and use professional-grade, well-maintained equipment for every activity.

Meet Our Pilot

Daniel Stiers — Professional Pilot & Instructor

Our director, Daniel Stiers, is a professional jet ski racing pilot who competes at the highest national and international level. He brings championship-level expertise to every lesson and excursion at Marbella JetSki.

Daniel's competition highlights include Campeón de España 2024 (circuito, rallyjet, offshore), Campeón de Andalucía 2025, and a podium finish at the Basquet Cup International Offshore (Getaria).

As a professional pilot, Daniel offers private racing lessons for customers who want to experience competition-level jet ski handling. His racing class — Runabout GP2 — uses the same high-performance jet skis available at Marbella JetSki.

The Racing Journey

From growing up on the Mediterranean waves to conquering the Spanish National Championships

1998

Family Legacy Begins

The Stiers family establishes STIERS E HIJOS S.L. in Marbella, laying the foundation for what would become the Costa del Sol's premier water sports destination.

2000s

Growing Up on the Water

Young Daniel spends his childhood on jet skis, learning every nuance of the Mediterranean. Natural talent meets relentless practice.

2020

Quality Certification

Marbella JetSki achieves ISO 9001:2015 & 14001:2015 certification, proving commitment to quality and environmental excellence.

2023

First Year Competing

Daniel enters his first competitive season with the RFEM, earning a 3rd place finish — an impressive debut that signals the champion to come.

2024

National Champion — 1st Place

National Champion with 4 gold medals at the Campeonato de España and Copa del Rey. Daniel claims 1st Place in Offshore, Circuit, and Rallyjet disciplines with 7 total medals.

2025

Andalusia Champion + National Podium

Campeón de Andalucía 2025 and 3rd Overall National — Daniel continues to dominate at regional level while competing at the top of the national stage.

NOW

Training the Next Generation

Today, Daniel combines his championship expertise with his family's 25+ years of experience to offer world-class training and unforgettable water experiences.
