Safety Rules & Regulations

The following rules apply to all participants. By booking an activity, you agree to comply with these rules. Failure to do so may result in immediate termination of the activity without refund.

1. General Safety Rules

  1. You must strictly follow all instructions provided by staff for the entire duration of your activity.
  2. The designated navigation area is indicated with four red buoys/floaters. It is absolutely forbidden to operate outside this zone.
  3. You must always remain at least 200 metres from shore and 50 metres from other jet skis or boats.
  4. You will be accompanied by an instructor/support boat at all times.
  5. In case of mechanical issues, pull the kill-switch cord to stop the engine. Wave your arms toward the instructor boat. Do NOT leave the jet ski.
  6. The use of jet skis is not permitted for anyone under 16 without parental authorisation.
  7. Taking a mobile phone into the water is strictly forbidden.
  8. All participants must wear the provided ISO 12402-5 life jacket and CE-rated helmet at all times.

2. Mandatory Safety Briefing

Before any activity, all participants must complete a 10-15 minute safety briefing covering:

  • Jet ski handling and controls
  • IBR (Intelligent Braking & Reverse) system operation
  • Kill-switch lanyard usage
  • Navigation rules and restricted zones
  • Emergency procedures and distress signals
  • AEMET weather conditions for the session

3. Equipment & Safety Measures

  • All jet skis are 2024-2025 models with IBR braking systems
  • ISO 12402-5 certified life jackets provided
  • CE-rated helmets provided
  • Support boat with qualified skipper posted within 300 metres
  • AED defibrillator available on-site
  • First aid kit on all support vessels
  • Daily mechanical inspections with up-to-date ITB certification
  • Weather verified against AEMET and State Ports services before each session

4. Accidents & Damage

In case of accidental damage to equipment caused by negligence or breach of rules:

  • The participant must cover repair expenses at prevailing market rates.
  • An accident declaration form must be completed with all required details.
  • Loss of key: €25 charge.

5. Media & Photography

Our staff may take photographs and videos during activities for promotional purposes. By participating, you may be asked to authorise the use of such media. This authorisation is optional and may be withdrawn at any time by contacting us.
