Why Marbella JetSki?

The Difference Is Clear

We're not just another rental company — we're the Costa del Sol's most trusted water sports centre

GPS-Tracked Fleet (OtoTrak)

Every jet ski is live-tracked via OtoTrak GPS for real-time monitoring. Our operations centre tracks speed, position, and perimeter — ensuring total safety at all times.

Premium Jet Ski Experiences

We combine safety, quality, and excitement to deliver the best jet ski experience on the Costa del Sol. Professional equipment, expert team, and unforgettable memories.

Maritime Qualified Team

Our entire team holds maritime safety qualifications. Every ride includes a mandatory safety briefing, certified equipment, and a support boat within 300m.

Our Story

About Us

Family Business Since 1998

Marbella JetSki (STIERS E HIJOS S.L.) has been the Costa del Sol's trusted water sports centre since 1998. We offer jet ski rides, coastal excursions, yacht charters, water sports, and professional racing lessons.

Our director, Daniel Stiers, is also a professional jet ski racing pilot — 4x Spanish National Champion, Andalusia Champion, and Copa del Rey medallist. He offers private racing lessons for customers looking for the ultimate experience.

Campeón de España 2024 Circuito, Rallyjet & Offshore (RFEM)
Campeón de Andalucía 2025 Runabout GP2
2nd — Basquet Cup International Offshore Racing, Getaria
ISO 9001 & 14001 Quality & Environmental Certified

Have Questions?

Frequently Asked Questions

To ride a jet ski, you need to be at least 18 years old, or 16 with signed parental consent. For family activities like pedal boats and banana boats, children can participate when accompanied by an adult.

No licence is required! We provide a comprehensive safety briefing before every activity. Our jet skis are easy to operate, and our instructors will ensure you're comfortable before heading out.

Cancel 24+ hours in advance for a full refund or free reschedule. Less than 24 hours notice incurs a 50% charge. Weather-related cancellations are always 100% refunded. See our full cancellation policy.

Yes! All activities include Civil Liability Insurance. This covers third-party injuries and property damage during normal use. Please note that damage caused by negligence or failure to follow safety instructions may result in repair charges at market rates.

We accept credit/debit cards (Visa, Mastercard), bank transfer, and cash on-site.

Safety comes first. Our qualified staff monitor AEMET weather data before every session. If conditions are unsafe, we'll reschedule you free of charge or issue a 100% refund — no questions asked. The decision is made by our team and is final.

Our main season runs from May to October, with peak availability during summer months. We may operate on good weather days during off-season — contact us to check availability!

Just bring swimwear, sunscreen, a towel, and a valid ID (passport or national ID for jet ski riders). We provide all safety equipment including life jackets and helmets. We recommend water shoes and bringing a change of clothes. Leave your phone on shore — it's not permitted in the water.

Yes! Professional photos and videos of your experience are available with every activity. Our staff capture the action so you can focus on the fun. With your consent, we may also use images for promotional purposes.

We're located at Playa de las Dunas, Urbanización Pinomar, Marbella (exit at km 189.5 on the AP-7). Free street parking is available nearby. We're directly on the beach — look for the Marbella JetSki flags! Get directions on Google Maps.

Absolutely! We cater for stag/hen parties, birthdays, corporate events, and large groups. Our group activities (banana boat, crazy sofa, donut ride) are priced per person (€20/pp), and we offer yacht charters for up to 12 guests. Contact us on WhatsApp for custom group packages.

We strongly recommend booking in advance during peak season (June–September) as slots fill up fast. Walk-ins are welcome when availability allows. You can book online, via WhatsApp, by phone, or in person at our beach location.

Safety is our top priority. Every participant receives a 10-15 minute mandatory safety briefing covering handling, hand signals, navigation rules, and emergency procedures. We provide ISO 12402-5 life jackets and CE helmets, and our 2024-2025 Yamaha VX jet skis are GPS-tracked (OtoTrak) with automatic power cut-off. A professional support boat with a qualified skipper is always within 300m, equipped with a first aid kit. We also monitor real-time weather conditions via State Ports and AEMET before every session.

Our entire team holds maritime safety qualifications. Owner Daniel Stiers is a 4x Spanish National Champion (RFEM 2024). All craft undergo daily mechanical inspections and hold up-to-date ITB (technical boat inspection) certifications. We are ISO 9001 (quality) and ISO 14001 (environmental) certified by OCA Global, and full equipment checks are performed after every use.
