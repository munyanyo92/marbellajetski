What to Expect

Your Lesson Experience

Every racing lesson is a private, one-on-one session with Daniel Stiers. Here's what's included.

Safety Briefing

Full safety briefing, life jacket, CE helmet, and all required gear provided before you ride.

Personal Coaching

1-on-1 instruction from a professional racing pilot. Technique tailored to your level and goals.

Competition Jet Ski

Ride the same Sea-Doo RXP-X 300 class that Daniel races. Professional grade, fully maintained.

Circuit & Open Water

Practice on a sheltered circuit for technique, then graduate to open water for real riding experience.

GoPro Recording

Available on Racing Experience and included with the Masterclass. Take home footage of your ride.

All Levels Welcome

No experience needed for the Basic lesson. Daniel adapts every session to match your skill level.

The Machine

Sea-Doo RXP-X 300 Modified

Daniel's competition machine — professionally tuned for championship-level performance.

ECU Stage 2 Remap

Custom engine management calibration removing factory limiters for maximum output across the full RPM range.

1630cc Supercharged — ~350 HP

Rotax 1630 ACE with upgraded supercharger. Approximate output ~350 HP (configuration-dependent). 0–100 km/h in approximately 3 seconds.

Riva Racing Sponsons

Extended rear sponsons for aggressive cornering grip and stability at competition speeds through tight buoy turns.

Riva Racing Intake & Impeller

High-flow intake grate and performance impeller for maximum thrust and acceleration out of every turn.

Riva Racing Handlebar

Extended and reinforced handlebar for superior leverage and control during high-speed manoeuvres and standing riding.

Competition Anti-Slip Seat

Race-spec anti-slip seat cover and deck pads for maximum rider connection through rough water and hard cornering.

Performance figures are approximate and depend on specific configuration, conditions, and rider weight. Modifications are for Daniel's personal competition machine.
